Bio

Scott, guitarist, arranger, and educator

I write and perform guitar-based music across acoustic and electric styles, with a focus on practical growth and meaningful repetition habits. My approach is simple: practical exercises first, style second, performance always.

This site is the home for notes, practice ideas, songs, and tools that I use in my own work as both performer and teacher.
